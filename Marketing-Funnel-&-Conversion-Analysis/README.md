**Task 03: Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis - Power BI Dashboard**

**📌 Project Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing marketing funnel performance and customer conversion behavior using Power BI. The goal is to understand how users move through different funnel stages — from initial contact to final subscription — and identify key drop-off points, high-performing segments, and campaign optimization opportunities.

The insights generated from this dashboard help businesses improve marketing efficiency, optimize sales strategy, and increase conversion rates.

**🎯 Business Objectives**
Analyze the end-to-end marketing funnel

Identify drop-off stages

Measure conversion rates at each stage

Evaluate campaign & channel effectiveness

Segment customers based on demographics & behavior

Provide actionable business recommendations

**📂 Dataset Description**

The dataset contains marketing campaign data from a banking marketing campaign, including:

Customer Attributes: age, job, education, marital status

Campaign Attributes: contact type, duration, campaign count, previous outcome

Target Variable: subscription (converted / not converted)

Total Records: 4,521

**📈 Key Business Insights**
Overall conversion rate is low (11.52%), indicating high drop-off in the funnel.

Maximum drop-off occurs between interested leads and final conversion stage.

Cellular contact channel delivers the highest conversions.

25–44 age group shows the highest subscription likelihood.

Excessive follow-ups (campaign > 3) significantly reduce conversions.

Short and effective calls perform better than long conversations.

**💡 Business Recommendations**

Focus marketing efforts on high-performing age groups (25–44).

Prioritize mobile-based campaigns.

Optimize call scripts for short, impactful conversations.

Limit follow-up attempts to 3–4 campaigns.

Increase marketing spend during high-conversion months (Oct–Dec).
